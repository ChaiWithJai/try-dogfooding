# Generate Ad Variants

Look for product specs in `data/specs.md` or any pasted context.

Based on the provided specs:
1. Identify the target audience and their core pain point.
2. Draft 3 variations of Meta ad copy (Short, Punchy, Image-focused).
3. Draft 2 variations of LinkedIn ad copy (Story-driven, Professional, Long-form).

Make sure each variation has a distinct hook and angle. Follow all voice and tone rules specified in CLAUDE.md.
